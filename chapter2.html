Chapter Two

What My Heart
Notices

little things that stay

You probably don't realize it,
but there are little things about you that stay in my mind.

The way you smile in photos — quietly, like you're
keeping a beautiful secret.

The way ordinary moments become memorable
just because you were part of them.

The way you make people feel comfortable
simply by being yourself.

Some things are difficult to explain.
They are simply felt.

— with love ❤️
a favourite moment
you, just being you
a moment in motion
softly remembered

there is still more to say…
