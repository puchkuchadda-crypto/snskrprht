What My Heart
Notices

little things that stay

❝
You probably don't realise it,
but there are little things about you
that stay in my mind.

The way you smile in photographs —
quietly, like you're keeping a beautiful secret.

The way ordinary moments become memorable
just because you were part of them.

The way you make people feel comfortable
simply by being yourself.

Some things are difficult to explain.
They are simply felt.
with love ❤️
archived moments
Fragments of you
a favourite moment
you, just being you
a moment in motion
softly remembered

there is still more to say…
