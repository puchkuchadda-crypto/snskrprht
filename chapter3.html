Chapter Three

Things I Never
Say Enough

words that live in my heart

Sometimes there are things that stay in the heart
longer than they stay on the lips.

I don't always say them.
Maybe because I never find the perfect words.
Maybe because some feelings are too genuine
to fit inside a simple sentence.

But if there is one thing I want you to know —

your presence has made ordinary days feel a little brighter,
simple moments feel a little warmer,
and memories feel a little more meaningful.

You are someone worth every unsaid word.
Every quiet thought. Every soft smile.
Every single time my heart said "her."
