Chapter Three

Things I Never
Say Enough

words that live where silence breathes
✦ ✦   ✦   ✦ ✦

Some things are too tender for the tongue —
they live deeper, in the part of the chest
that tightens when you walk into a room.

I don't always say them.
Not because the feeling is small —
but because some truths are too wide
to fit through the narrow door of words.

But if these pages could carry just one thing,
it would be this —

You changed the texture of ordinary days.
The light fell differently.
Silence felt less empty.
Coffee tasted warmer.

Every quiet thought that ended in your name.
Every song that suddenly made sense.
Every single moment my heart whispered —
softly, with certainty —
“her.”

always & forever ❤
